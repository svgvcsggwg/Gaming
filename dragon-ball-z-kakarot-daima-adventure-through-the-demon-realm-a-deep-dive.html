DRAGON BALL Z: KAKAROT DAIMA: Adventure Through the Demon Realm - A Deep Dive

By Felix
March 2, 2025

DRAGON BALL Z: KAKAROT, the action RPG developed by CyberConnect2 and published by Bandai Namco Entertainment, has captivated fans by allowing them to relive the iconic story of Goku and the Z-Fighters. Known for its epic battles, faithful recreation of the anime's storyline, and engaging gameplay, DRAGON BALL Z: KAKAROT has sold over 8 million copies. Now, the game is expanding its universe with a brand-new DLC pack: "DAIMA: Adventure Through the Demon Realm." This DLC ties directly into the recent Dragon Ball Daima anime series, offering players a chance to experience a fresh, exciting adventure with a unique twist – a miniature Goku.

This article provides a detailed overview about everything we currently know of the New DRAGON BALL Z: KAKAROT DAIMA DLC, while drawing parallels to the Dragon Ball Daima Anime and answering a few burning questions in regards to the entire saga.

The Premise: A Journey into the Demon Realm

The "DAIMA: Adventure Through the Demon Realm" DLC plunges players into the storyline of Dragon Ball Daima. The core concept of Daima revolves around a mysterious conspiracy that results in Goku and his friends being transformed into children. This transformation weakens them considerably, setting the stage for a new kind of challenge. To undo this curse, Goku and his companions must journey to a new world: the Demon Realm.

The DLC promises to faithfully recreate the "Third Demon Realm," as depicted in the Dragon Ball Daima anime. This includes iconic landscapes with features like "floating islands and mysterious Skyseed plants." Players will explore these intricate environments, encountering new allies and enemies, while navigating the challenges of this mysterious world.

Gameplay: New Mechanics and a Nostalgic Twist

The Kakarot Daima DLC doesn't just offer a new story; it introduces fresh gameplay mechanics that reflect the unique circumstances of the Daima narrative:

  • Goku (Mini) and the Power Pole: Players will primarily control Goku in his miniature form, referred to as "Goku (Mini)." This smaller stature necessitates a change in combat style. Embracing the nostalgia of early Dragon Ball, Goku (Mini) will utilize the Power Pole (Nyoibo) in battle, a weapon not prominently featured in Dragon Ball Z.
  • New Techniques and Support Characters:. "Players can also expect a new battle experience thanks to the introduction of elements unique to the DLC such as the Power Pole or the new form of Goku (mini) Super Saiyan." Also the DLC, leveraging Goku's allies in creative ways. Characters like Glorio, Shin, and Panzy provide support, utilizing support skills that enchance your skills through support and strategic combat advantages.
  • Z Combos: Goku’s Friends provide supporting attacks, and the combined power of the team create devistating Z Combo Finishing Attacks.

Super Saiyan Transformation

Despite his reduced size, Goku (Mini) retains the ability to transform into a Super Saiyan, adding a powerful dimension to the combat encounters.

Story Details and Characters: Echoes of Daima

The DLC is designed to mirror the *Dragon Ball Daima* anime's plot. Here's a breakdown of what players can expect:

  • A Two-Part Adventure: The story is a direct adaptation of the anime series, in the game, *Dragon Ball Daima*. The DLC story will follow the "DAIMA story."
  • The Third Demon Realm: Players will get to explore a whole new world for them to traverse in. A recreation of the Third Demon Realm featured in the Anime, the new maps will host various regions including; floating Islands and skyseed plants.
  • New Companions: Players will relive iconic moments from the perspective of Goku (Mini) through his many advantures, accompained by allies like Glorio, Shin, and Panzy.
  • Reliving Iconic Adventures: The DLC story mode adapts moments directly from the series.

Release Date and Availability

The "DAIMA: Adventure Through the Demon Realm" DLC is being released in a phased approach:

  • Part 1: Is scheduled to launch between July and September 2025 (Summer 2025).
  • Part 2: Will follow, with a release window of between January and March 2026.
  • Bonus Pack: It released on February 27 and Included Consumable itmes for those purchased the DLC as a Season Pass, including:
    • Ultimate Meat Feast
    • Mega Sacred Water
    • Purple Dino Horn
    • Awakening Water

DRAGON BALL Z: KAKAROT is currently available on a wide range of platforms, ensuring broad accessibility for the DLC:

  • PlayStation 5
  • PlayStation 4
  • Xbox Series X/S
  • Xbox One
  • Nintendo Switch
  • PC (via Steam)

Dragon Ball Daima: The Anime Connection

To fully appreciate the DLC, it's essential to understand its source material, Dragon Ball Daima. Daima is an anime series that bridges the gap between the end of Dragon Ball Z (after the defeat of Kid Buu) and the beginning of Dragon Ball Super.

Here's a quick recap of Daima Story as Described by Creator:

"Hello. I'm Akira Toriyama. I'm currently working on a new Dragon Ball. The Title is “Dragon Ball DAIMA”. “DAIMA” is a made-up term, which in Japanese characters would be “大魔” or in English would be something like “Evil.” Due to a conspiracy, Goku and his friends are turned small. In order to fix things, they'll head off to a new world! It's a grand adventure with intense action in an unknown and mysterious world. Since Goku has to make up for his petite size, he uses his Nyoibo (Power Pole) to fight, something not seen in a long time. I came up with the story and settings, as well as a lot of the designs.

The Story Starts where Goku and company were peacefully when, were suddenly attacked, and turned to into children as a resut of the assult, in which a Demon Realm King set in Motion.. With their Power reduced Goku and Z fighters Travel to Demon World, hoping to return back to Normal by Finding a solution that will restore everyone. Players Explore Third Demon Realm, which include, The Floating Islands, and The Skyseed Plants. The Game also Introduces Players to Three new Allies in Goku's Adventures, Shin, Panzy, Glorio.

CyberConnect2: The Masters of Anime Adaptations

The developer behind *DRAGON BALL Z: KAKAROT*, CyberConnect2, has a long and respected history of creating high-quality anime-based video games. Their expertise extends beyond the *Dragon Ball* universe and includes titles such as:

  • The .hack series
  • Little Tail Bronx series
  • Naruto: Ultimate Ninja Storm series
  • JoJo's Bizarre Adventure: All Star Battle
  • JoJo's Bizarre Adventure: Eyes of Heaven
  • Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba – The Hinokami Chronicles
  • Fuga: Melodies of Steel series

This impressive track record demonstrates CyberConnect2's ability to faithfully translate the essence of beloved anime series into engaging video game experiences. Their involvement with DRAGON BALL Z: KAKAROT and this new DLC assures fans of a quality product that respects the source material.

The Future of DRAGON BALL Z: KAKAROT

The "DAIMA: Adventure Through the Demon Realm" DLC represents a significant expansion of the *DRAGON BALL Z: KAKAROT* universe. Given the game's success and the ongoing support from Bandai Namco, it's likely that further content could be developed in the future. Dragon Ball is going throug a Revival and we might continue see Dragon Ball Z: Kakarot through Future content packs for years to come.

Conclusion: A Must-Have for Dragon Ball Fans

The "DAIMA: Adventure Through the Demon Realm" DLC for *DRAGON BALL Z: KAKAROT* offers a compelling combination of a new story, fresh gameplay mechanics, and a faithful adaptation of the *Dragon Ball Daima* anime. Whether you're a longtime *Dragon Ball* fan or a newcomer to the franchise, this DLC promises a thrilling adventure that expands the world of *DRAGON BALL Z: KAKAROT* in exciting ways. With its phased release starting in Summer 2025, players have ample time to prepare for their journey into the Demon Realm.
