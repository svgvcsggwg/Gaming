Leaked Details: Samsung Galaxy Tab S10 FE Plus Emerges with a 13.1-inch Display 

By Felix
March 1, 2025



The Samsung Galaxy Tab S10 FE Plus is making headlines after recent leaks have revealed exciting details about the upcoming tablet. As Samsung prepares to expand its mid-range tablet lineup, enthusiasts have been treated to fresh images and preliminary specifications that promise a notable upgrade over previous models. Among the most eye-catching revelations is the news that the Plus variant will sport a massive 13.1-inch display, potentially setting a new benchmark for affordable, large-screen tablets.



A Bold Leap in Display Size



According to the leaked information, the Galaxy Tab S10 FE Plus is expected to feature a 13.1-inch display. This is a significant jump from its predecessor, the Galaxy Tab S9 FE Plus, which reportedly came with a 12.4-inch screen. The larger display is likely to offer a more immersive viewing experience, especially for users who prefer a tablet that performs well in landscape mode for tasks such as streaming, gaming, or multitasking. The design emphasis on slim bezels further suggests that Samsung is committed to maximizing the screen-to-body ratio without compromising on aesthetics.



The front image that surfaced in the leak shows a design with slim bezels and a centered selfie camera. This layout not only contributes to a modern look but also ensures that the device offers a balanced visual experience. Users holding the tablet in landscape mode will likely appreciate the symmetry and minimal distractions along the edges.



Certification Clues and Model Numbers



One of the key aspects of this leak comes from information published on SafetyKorea, a reputable certification website. The listing reveals two new model numbers: SM-X620 and SM-X620N. Experts speculate that these identifiers correspond to the Galaxy Tab S10 FE Plus and its South Korean variant, respectively. Although Samsung has yet to officially confirm these details, the appearance of these model numbers on the certification site adds credibility to the leak.



The presence of distinct model numbers for regional variants indicates that Samsung is preparing tailored versions of the tablet for different markets. Such segmentation is common practice for global tech brands, ensuring that local regulations and market demands are met efficiently.



Design Insights and Usability



Beyond the impressive screen size, the leaked images provide a glimpse into the overall design of the Galaxy Tab S10 FE Plus. The device appears to incorporate minimal bezels, a design element that is both modern and functional. Slim bezels not only enhance the visual appeal but also contribute to a more ergonomic device—especially when held in landscape mode for productivity tasks.



The centered selfie camera is another design highlight. Its placement suggests that Samsung has carefully considered user ergonomics, ensuring that the camera performs well during video calls and selfies without interfering with the overall aesthetic. The balanced design of the tablet is expected to provide a comfortable experience whether for work or entertainment.



Positioning in the Galaxy Tab S10 FE Series



The Galaxy Tab S10 FE series is poised to offer consumers a choice between a standard model and a Plus variant. While the standard model is rumored to maintain a 10.9-inch display—consistent with last year’s design—the Plus model pushes the envelope with its larger 13.1-inch screen. This differentiation allows Samsung to cater to diverse consumer preferences, offering a more compact option for those who value portability and a larger one for multimedia enthusiasts.



In addition to the display differences, the two models might also vary in terms of other specifications such as battery capacity, processor performance, and camera quality. However, for now, the most striking distinction lies in the screen size, positioning the Galaxy Tab S10 FE Plus as a compelling option for users looking for an affordable yet expansive tablet experience.



What’s Next for Samsung’s Mid-Range Tablets?



Although no detailed specifications beyond the display size and design have been officially released, the leaks hint at a number of potential upgrades. Rumors suggest that the Galaxy Tab S10 FE Plus could be powered by a modern mid-range chipset, offering up to 12GB of RAM and 256GB of storage. These improvements would provide a significant performance boost compared to previous generations, ensuring smooth multitasking and efficient handling of everyday tasks.



Industry insiders also speculate that Samsung may equip the new tablet with an upgraded camera system. While the leaked image clearly shows a centered selfie camera, it is expected that the front camera will feature enhanced capabilities compared to the 8MP units found in earlier models. This upgrade would be particularly attractive for video conferencing and content creation, areas that have become increasingly important in today’s digital age.



Market Implications and Consumer Expectations



With the increasing demand for larger screens and enhanced multimedia experiences, Samsung’s decision to launch a 13.1-inch Galaxy Tab S10 FE Plus appears to be well-timed. As consumers continue to shift from traditional laptops to versatile tablets for both work and entertainment, the importance of a large, high-quality display cannot be overstated.



The leaked design and specifications are expected to spark interest among tech enthusiasts and general consumers alike. In markets where affordability is key, the Galaxy Tab S10 FE Plus could become a popular choice by striking the right balance between performance and price. The introduction of a larger display in an affordable package might also force competitors to reconsider their offerings in the mid-range tablet segment.



Furthermore, the upcoming launch of this device may also pave the way for additional software enhancements. With Samsung’s One UI already known for its user-friendly interface and regular updates, consumers can likely expect a seamless integration of hardware and software, resulting in a cohesive user experience.



Final Thoughts



Although Samsung has not yet confirmed the full list of specifications for the Galaxy Tab S10 FE Plus, the leaked details have generated a significant buzz. The promise of a 13.1-inch display combined with a sleek design featuring slim bezels and a centered selfie camera positions the tablet as an exciting contender in the mid-range market.



As the launch date draws nearer, more details will undoubtedly emerge. For now, the certification data from SafetyKorea and the accompanying images provide a tantalizing glimpse into what could be one of Samsung’s most ambitious mid-range tablets yet. If the rumored specifications hold true, the Galaxy Tab S10 FE Plus could offer consumers an unprecedented combination of performance, design, and affordability.



What to Expect Moving Forward



In the coming weeks, industry watchers will be on high alert for further leaks and official announcements from Samsung. It is anticipated that the device will be unveiled alongside other new tablets in the Galaxy Tab S10 FE series, offering both Wi-Fi and 5G variants to cater to a wide range of user needs. With its impressive design and promising specifications, the Galaxy Tab S10 FE Plus is set to challenge current market leaders and redefine expectations for budget-friendly tablets.



Stay tuned for more updates as we continue to track the evolution of Samsung’s mid-range tablet lineup. For now, the leaks have set the stage for what could be a major leap forward in design and functionality for the Galaxy Tab S10 FE Plus.
