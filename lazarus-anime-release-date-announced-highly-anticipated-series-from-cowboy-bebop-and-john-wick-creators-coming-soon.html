Lazarus Anime Release Date Announced: Highly Anticipated Series from Cowboy Bebop and John Wick Creators Coming Soon

By Felix
March 1, 2025

Anime fans worldwide are eagerly awaiting the arrival of Lazarus, a brand new anime series that promises to be a landmark event in 2025. Adult Swim has officially announced the release date for this highly anticipated project, created by visionary minds behind iconic works like Cowboy Bebop and John Wick.

In a thrilling update for cinema and television enthusiasts, Adult Swim unveiled the first official trailer for Lazarus, offering a tantalizing glimpse into the anime's stunning visuals and intricate storyline. The trailer, which showcases key moments and characters, culminates in the reveal of the premiere date: April 5, 2025. Fans will be able to catch new episodes on Adult Swim starting on this date, with the series becoming available for streaming on Max the following day, April 6, 2025.

Lazarus: A Gripping Story of Hope and Deception

Set in the year 2052, Lazarus unfolds in a world transformed by a medical miracle. A revolutionary drug has been discovered, promising to eradicate all disease and ushering in an era of unprecedented health and longevity. However, this utopian vision is shattered three years later when the very scientist who developed this life-altering drug reveals a shocking truth: the medication is, in fact, a slow-acting poison. Designed to eventually lead to the demise of everyone who has taken it, the drug's sinister nature is now exposed, plunging the world into chaos and despair.

 

In response to this impending global catastrophe, a specialized team of five individuals is assembled. Codenamed "Lazarus," this elite group is tasked with a critical mission: to locate the enigmatic doctor, the mastermind behind the deadly drug, and thwart his catastrophic plan to "save the world" through mass poisoning. The anime promises a thrilling race against time as the Lazarus team navigates a world on the brink of collapse, grappling with complex moral dilemmas and facing formidable adversaries in their quest to uncover the truth and secure humanity's future.

A Stellar Creative Team Behind the Project

Lazarus boasts an impressive pedigree of creative talent, uniting masterminds from both the anime and action film industries. The anime is directed and produced by the acclaimed Shinichiro Watanabe, the visionary behind genre-defining anime series such as Cowboy Bebop and Samurai Champloo. Watanabe is renowned for his signature blend of stylistic animation, compelling narratives, and unforgettable characters, and fans are eager to see his unique touch applied to Lazarus.

Adding another layer of excitement to the project is the involvement of Chad Stahelski, the director of the blockbuster John Wick film series. Stahelski, celebrated for his expertise in crafting visceral and innovative action sequences, has contributed to the creation of the action scenes in Lazarus. This collaboration promises to deliver breathtaking and dynamic action within the anime, setting a new standard for visual storytelling in the medium.

The animation production for Lazarus is being handled by the renowned MAPPA Studio, in collaboration with Sola Entertainment. MAPPA is currently at the forefront of the anime industry, responsible for producing some of the most critically acclaimed and commercially successful anime in recent years. Their portfolio includes the globally popular Attack on Titan (Season 4), Jujutsu Kaisen, Vinland Saga (Season 2), Chainsaw Man, and Hell's Paradise: Jigokuraku. With MAPPA's exceptional animation quality and Sola Entertainment's expertise, Lazarus is poised to be a visually stunning and technically impressive anime series.

Get Ready for Lazarus in April 2025

With its intriguing premise, stellar creative team, and stunning animation, Lazarus is shaping up to be one of the most anticipated anime releases of 2025. Mark your calendars for April 5, 2025, for the Adult Swim premiere, and be ready to stream it on Max starting April 6, 2025. The countdown has begun for this groundbreaking anime experience.
